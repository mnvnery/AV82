--- category: commercial layout: film-project title: Alvaro Siza Vieira - Marrocos - EM VIAGEM - Mais que Arquitetura synopsis: "

Curated by Luís Urbano, we produced a series of interviews that served to promote the exhibition “Mais que Arquitetura” at Fundação Marques da Silva. These explored the work of several important personalities of the history of Portuguese architecture such as Álvaro Siza Vieira and Bartolomeu Costa Cabral. The work was shot entirely at the Palacete Lopes Martins, the venue of the exhibition.
