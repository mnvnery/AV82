Music video for the song “Dançar, até que a noite caia” by artist Carlos Moisés, lead singer of the Portuguese band “Quinta do Bill”. Besides the video's creative orientation, the team was responsible for production, directing, cinematography and post-production. The project was entirely made in our studio in the city of Porto.
